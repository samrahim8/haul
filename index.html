About

Hi, I'm Sam Rahim.

I'm a full-stack operator. I run SR Ventures - a small studio where I build + grow businesses.

I'm from Southern California, and lived there for my first 25 years. When I was in college at UC Irvine, I spent a summer in the jungles of Belize doing archaeology - our team found dozens of artifacts + structures that were thousands of years old. I also got my first gig as an intern at an LA-based startup - Lokal Travel. That was the most valuable experience of my life, and the foundation for everything I was able to take on after.

My experience at Lokal helped me get an internship (and eventually a full-time role) at Disney's corporate campus in Glendale - where, ultimately, I met my wife.

Since then, I've worked for several high-growth startups - Guild, Street FC, Switchyards - and have lived in five different states. Now, my wife and I are based in Orlando, and I'm building + advising businesses.
